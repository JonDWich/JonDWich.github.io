About Me

Hello! My name is Jon, a 4th year computer science student with a penchant for video game design and systems programming.

I have four years of experience working on complex modifications to existing games, such as turning an action platformer into a Roguelike, overhauling a character controller to support physics, extending combat systems, and writing a framework for animation playback.
I also have experience with testing games during development, including the commercially released Gravity Circuit.

Below you'll find short overviews of the skills used and difficulties faced in these projects, as well as links to more detailed writeups.
